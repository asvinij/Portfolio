Brand Training

US Brands

Business Problem:

The US Talent Acquisition team in India lacked familiarity with US brands and the specific roles and responsibilities tied to those brands. This knowledge gap led to difficulties in candidate sourcing and assessments.

Approach:

The training covered 45 popular US brands, divided into three stages (15 brands per stage). I implemented a game-based learning approach, using engaging mechanics to facilitate brand recall and role association, making the learning process both interactive and effective.

Results:

Post-training, 90% of the TA team members were able to recall 80% of the US brands covered in the training—6 months later, demonstrating the lasting impact of the training on knowledge retention and recall.
